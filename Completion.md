## Moves

- Moves without secondary effect
    - Gust
    - Horn Attack
    - Peck
    - Scratch
    - Tackle
    - Vine Whip
    - Water Gun

- Moves with high critical
    - Karate Chop

- Moves with high priorities
    - Quick Attack (+1)

- Moves hitting N times in one turn
    - Double kick (2-2)
    - Fury Attack (2-5)

- Moves inflicting statuses
    - Confusion (confusion)
    - Ember (burn)
    - Hyper Fang (flinch)
    - Leech Seed (seed)
    - Low Kick (flinch)
    - Poison Powder (poison)
    - Poison Sting (poison)
    - Sleep Powder (sleep)
    - Stun Spore (paralysis)
    - Thunder Shock (paralysis, except Electric types)
    - Thunder Wave (paralysis)

- Moves dropping target stats by one stage
    - Bubble (speed)
    - Growl (attack)
    - Leer (defense)
    - Sand Attack (accuracy)
    - String Shot (speed)
    - Tail Whip (defense)

- Moves dropping target stats by two stages
    - Screech (defense)

- Moves raising user stats by one stage
    - Defense curl (defense)
    - Double Team (evasion)
    - Harden (defense)

- Special moves
    - Bide (activate turn 1, wait 1-2 turns, and unleash last turn double damage taken while waiting)
    - Struggle (launched with 0pp, recoil damage)
